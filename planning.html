IT planning

Assistance with strategic IT planning is one of most frequently requested services. We think of strategic IT as a core competitive strength, helping you to invest in the right technologies to prepare your company today for tomorrow’s challenges.

NTP IT consultants will work with you to document a detailed strategic plan that maps your business goals to short-term and long-term action plans to achieve those goals.

IT Budgeting

IT budgeting goes hand in hand with strategic IT planning. But trying to build a technology budget without a clear understanding of what your company needs is frustrating.

NTP IT consultants work with you to develop a realistic IT budget that incorporates both your own assets and resources, plus the projected costs of the new technology that will deliver the most value to your organization and help you reach your business goals.

IT Assessments

Chances are your network has developed gradually over time, with layers of hardware and software that may now be contributing to unintended security gaps or inefficiencies.

NTP IT consultants can conduct a comprehensive review of your IT environment and examine how your current technology is helping or hindering your business. You receive a written evaluation of the key areas that need improvement, with prioritized recommendations.

Security Risk Assessments

As your business relies more heavily on technology, the inherent risks increase as well. A single data breach or ransomware attack can cause huge financial, legal and reputational damage. Our comprehensive cybersecurity assessment identifies your level of risk and defines a mitigation approach to minimize and manage your threats and vulnerabilities.

Cloud & Data Migration Services

Many businesses are transferring their data and applications from on-premises data centers to private or public clouds, typically to reduce costs, increase security, and build redundancy.Our IT consultancy helps you develop the right cloud migration strategy, including having the right cloud security in place. We take you through the pros and cons, help you evaluate cloud providers, calculate costs, and then implement a plan to ensure a successful cloud migration project.

Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery (BC/DR)

Business interruptions happen, and a continuity and disaster recovery plan is vital to keep your company up and running through whatever crisis you might face.

As an information technology consulting firm, NTP helps you formulate a BC/DR plan that encompasses how employees communicate in a crisis, where they go to find the information they need, and how they can access the tools and processes they need to perform their jobs in any situation.

Virtual CIO (vCIO)

A virtual CIO is a semi-dedicated IT executive who provides short- or long-term support for IT strategy, operations, and leadership. If your business needs strategic direction but you don’t have the budget for a full-time CIO, a virtual CIO is an excellent choice to help you set your priorities and manage your day-to-day technology operations.

Our seasoned virtual CIOs have helped tens of other companies navigate the same challenges you are facing.

Why Your Business Need Information Technology Planning Services?

One of the most significant reason, the key point and the value delivered by IT planning is that you can create an IT strategic plan or roadmap for your organization. In general, an IT strategic plan lays out your key business drivers, objectives and goals, and identifies the actions needed to achieve them.

An IT roadmap is crucial to ensure your technology organization is in alignment with the overall business strategy and objectives. Having a documented IT strategy benefits both technology leaders and functional executives by creating transparency and encouraging collaboration across teams.

More specifically, an IT strategic roadmap can help you:

⇨ Create an “end-state vision” that gives you and your team a clear picture of where your company is headed.
⇨ Identify priorities in both the short term and long term, so everyone understands what needs to be tackled first and what can wait.
⇨ Align your business plan to a realistic IT budget that enables you to implement the right level of innovation to meet your organization's needs.
⇨ Gain executive buy-in across all critical stakeholders, allowing for quicker and more efficient execution of the plan.
